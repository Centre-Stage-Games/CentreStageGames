Game Reviews From A High Schooler



SNES Games You Should Play

Personally, I like old videogames. I find them to be quite charming, and it's nice to look back on what has inspired the newer games that are coming out. Of all of the 32-bit and below consoles, my favorite is the Super Nintendo Entertainment System. This page is to share some of my favorite games and shed some light on some good obscure titles. I hope you enjoy.


Contra III: The Alien Wars

Contra III is a side-scrolling platformer that, like other contra games, is hard as nails. Your main weapon is a gun that you can upgrade temporarily with various power ups, but you can also use nukes. It has beautiful graphics and if you want a challenging platformer, this is one of the best on the console.

Donkey Kong Country

Donkey Kong Country is a light hearted side-scroller with good music, graphics and gameplay. You can play it by yourself or with a friend and there is a ton of levels so you're at least going to have a lot of content. If your looking for a fun coop game to play with a friend or for a fun platformer, I suggest playing this game.

Earthbound

Earthbound is a weird and wacky RPG that has inpired many modern RPGs. Unlike most RPGs, it takes place in a more modern setting, you're just a kid with psychic powers taking down slugs, the mafia, and everything in between. If you like RPGs and are looking for a change in setting or if you just want to play a fun RPG, Earthbound is a good pick.

F-Zero

F-Zero is a racing game with a fast pace, great music and good graphics. Sadly, it's only single player but it still holds up as a fun singleplayer experience. It's great for some on and off gameplay and if you like racing games, this is a great choice.

Ken Griffey Jr.
Presents Major League Baseball

This is honestly one of the best Baseball games I've played. I don't really like sports game with this being one of the only exceptions for me. It's great with friends and is a great experience.

Kirby Super Star

Imagine, if you will, 8 games, in 1 cartridge. Sounds impossible right? Kirby Super Star, manages to have essentialy has 5 kirby games and 3 minigames, all of which being incredibly fun. It is a great time by yourself or with friends.

Kirby's Dream Course

Kirby's Dream Course is a golf kirby game, which sounds odd but was really done well. I really like playing with friends, trying to steal each others stars and getting to the goal first. It's a great multiplayer and singleplayer experience.

Legend of Zelda:
A Link to the Past

I love this game. I have replayed it so much and I've speedrun randomizers of it. It has great puzzles, dungeons and secrets, just like other zelda games. It is a game I think everyone should play.

Mario's Super Picross

This game was only released in Japan but is on the switch's virtual console. It doesn't have an english translation but the controls are simple enough and this is the game that got me hooked on picross.

Mega Man X

Mega Man X is one of the absolute best platformers in general. It takes the classic mega man games, improves the controls and abilities, improves the levels and adds secrets in every level to help you get stronger. You can even unlock the hadoken from street fighter. This is a must-play on SNES.

Operation Logic Bomb

This is an obscure title that is honestly pretty solid. It focuses on a search and destroy format, having large levels for you to go through, taking down enemies and eventually the boss. I don't think this game gets as much love as they should.

Rock'N Roll Racing

This is a decent racing game that is fun with friends but really shows off in the music department, having songs like Paranoid, Bad To The Bone and Highway Star. It's pretty fun and enjoyable.

Spanky's Quest

Spanky's Quest is a puzzle platformer where you create a bubble that you can change thesize of to pop it and summon various sports equipment. Although it's weird it's a solid game if you can't decide want you want to play.

Star Fox

Star fox is awesome for being a 3d game on the SNES. It is a fun arcade-like shooter with multiple different pathways. It is an awesome fun game that you should play.

Street Fighter Alpha 2

This is one of the better fighting games, being the best port of the arcade game, with a ton of cool characters and good controls and gameplay. If you like fighting games, give this a shoot.

Super Castlevania IV

Super Castlevania IV has stunning graphics, a great spooky vibe and good platforming gameplay. It is a great run through and is a must-play on SNES.

Super Ghouls 'N Ghosts

This is an extremly hard platformer. If you're used to Mario and other platformers, good luck. The game is great but will need some practice and a lot of hours to master.

Super Mario Kart

It's mario kart, what were you expecting? Although the controls are a bit janky, it's great fun by yourself and with others.

Super Mario RPG:
Legend of the Seven Stars

One of the best RPGs ever made and is a great rendition of a combination of RPG and Mario. It's fun, lighthearted, and bowser is a playable character. It's very fun and awesome and is a must-play on SNES.

Super Mario World

Super Mario World is everything great about a Mario game. If you like Mario or if you just want to play a fun game, this is a must-play.

Super Mario World 2:
Yoshi's Island

Yoshi's Island is a fun platformer simmilar to mario but having distinct characteristics that makes it unique and still a great platfromer.

Super Metroid

Vibe, music and gameplay. this game has it all and more. It has great puzzles, bosses, art and abilities. I have speedran this game with a randomizer in combonation A Link To The Past. This is obviously a must-play.

Super Punch Out!!

It is short but it does have some great fights. I really enjoyed the first game on NES and this game makes it better. To many people rank it poorly.

Super Street Fighter 2

My favorite fighting game, it has great characters and stages and is just great. I love playing this with my dad because he used to play as a kid and is really tough to beat.

Ultimate Mortal Kombat 3

This used to be my go to game to play with friends. It has great characters and so many cool finshers. The blood is a great touch.

Wild Guns

Ths is a great combo between a side-scroller and a shooter. It is very arcady and is very fun. The wild west with robots theme is also pretty cool.
